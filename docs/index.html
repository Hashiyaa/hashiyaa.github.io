About Myself

I am currently an undergraduate majoring in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests include robotics, computer vision, machine learning, optimization, computer graphics, and potentially more. I am working with Professor Michael Gleicher and Professor Bilge Mutlu on several motion planning projects in the Collaborative Robotics Laboratory. A large part of my research experience has involved investigating and extending RelaxedIK, a framework previously developed in our lab for generating accurate and feasible robot arm motions in real-time. You can learn more about RelaxedIK here. In my leisure time, I love playing badminton, watching anime, playing Go, playing video games, designing video games, and developing video games.

I’m applying to graduate schools this semester!
